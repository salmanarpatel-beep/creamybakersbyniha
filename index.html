Welcome to Creamy Bakers By Niha

Every cake we make is a labour of love. We started as a small home kitchen in 2020 with one goal — to create desserts so good, they make your celebrations unforgettable.

We use only premium, carefully sourced ingredients — no preservatives, no shortcuts. Every bake is made fresh to order, tailored to your taste and occasion.

From elegant wedding tiers to whimsical birthday cakes and rich fudgy brownies, we pour our entire heart into every single creation.
